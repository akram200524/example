About us

An idea of selling this product is very interesting. In 2015, the director of the Ali pharmaceutical inc., by chance, having visited one of the hypermarkets in Tajikistan, got to the presentation of these products, and at that time he had an idea to organize wide spread sale of these products. A few years later, he had the opportunity to bring this idea to life. The question may arise as to what interested him in this product, and so for the production of natural " Mulberry bar" the berries are hand-picked on the slopes of the Pamir Mountains at an altitude of about 2000 meters above sea level.

"Mulberry bars" are absolutely natural, do not contain sugar, preservatives, dyes, flavors, gluten and GMOs. In the manufacture of bars do not use heat treatment, so these bars are preferable for rawatarians. Drying of berries take place in natural conditions. Addition of various ingredients (fruits, nut fractions) to the content of "Mulberry bars" made it possible to create a whole series of healthy products.

Based on the natural properties of mulberry the quality of production, as well as the exceptional taste of Mulberry bars are certified and got awards at many specialized exhibitions. This product has proven itself in the healthy food market and has become an integral part in the lives of many supporters of a healthy lifestyle.

The composition of mulberry also includes malic acid, tannins and pectin substances, vitamins C, P, carotene, choline. In addition, vitamins B1, B2, PP are supplemented with essential oils and lipids necessary for normal human life.

OUR MISSION

We strive to give a modern person an opportunity to live at a different quality level, where there is an understanding and awareness of the need to take care of health and improve the quality of life. Thus, supplying the domestic and foreign market with only natural products from ecologically clean regions of our planet, our company takes care of health and the future of our children and performs the function of society improvement.
