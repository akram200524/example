delivery

Dear partners, in order for the shipment and delivery of products to be carried out reliably and in accordance with the specifics of the transport company you have chosen, we recommend specifying the desired and necessary conditions for cargo transportation when ordering.

If the TC is not specified in the order, the cargo will be sent by one of the TC that the goods were sent to you earlier. We will independently deliver the cargo worth from 20 tons to the TC terminal in Moscow within the MKAD. When ordering for less than 20 tr., delivery to the TC terminal within the MKAD will be 500 rubles. Interterminal delivery at the expense of the Consignee. The cost and time of delivery to the shopping center located outside or in the MKAD area is agreed additionally. Daily flights are carried out to TC PEC, Business Lines, Baikal Service, further KIT, GlavDostavka, DAC, Byte Transit. Flights to other TC are carried out once or twice a week.

We carry out courier delivery of goods In Moscow within the MKAD at a cost of more than 20 tr. your account with the help of a partner transport company. When ordering for less than 20 t.p., the cost of delivery is included in the bill – 500 rubles. Please pay attention, loads weighing more than 20kg are delivered to the entrance! Couriers, as a rule, ask for an additional reward for lifting.

Most TC do not allow the shipment of any liquids without rigid packaging (ZHU) – crate or pallet board. If your order explicitly states the requirement to ship without a ZHU, we will try to do this, however, if it is impossible to carry out such transportation, part of the cargo will be sent to ZHU in accordance with the requirements of the TC. In the absence of explicit instructions in the order, fragile goods and liquids will be sent to ZHU. It should be taken into account that the TC PEC is a random number sensor on demand for liquids. We ask you to independently monitor the temperature conditions of transportation of products containing chocolate. In the warm season, it may melt during transportation, which is not a marriage, but may worsen the presentation.

We ask you to understand that after confirming and transferring your order to the assembly, it is impossible to change the order downwards. If it is necessary to remove any product from the confirmed order, we will be forced to stop its assembly, disband the order and start approving a new one. The time spent on this procedure is from 1-2 days or more! At the same time, you can add a product by making a new order and combining the sending of your orders.
