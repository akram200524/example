Mulberry bar

Natural product without preservatives, without artificial additives, sugarless. Suitable for rawatarians and vegans. Delicious and useful treat for children and adults

Company

An idea of selling this product is very interesting. In 2015, the director of the Ali pharmaceutical inc., by chance, having visited one of the hypermarkets in Tajikistan, got to the presentation of these products, and at that time he had an idea to organize wide spread sale of these products.

A few years later, he had the opportunity to bring this idea to life. The question may arise as to what interested him in this product, and so for the production of natural " Mulberry bar" the berries are hand-picked on the slopes of the Pamir Mountains at an altitude of about 2000 meters above sea level. "Mulberry bars" are absolutely natural, do not contain sugar, preservatives, dyes, flavors, gluten and GMOs.
